# Thematic Index

A comprehensive topical index showing where major themes appear throughout the Bible. Use this guide for topical studies across multiple books.

## A

### Atonement
- **Leviticus** - Sacrificial system and Day of Atonement
- **Isaiah 53** - Suffering Servant bears our sins
- **Romans** - Justification through Christ's blood
- **Hebrews** - Christ as perfect High Priest and final sacrifice
- **1 John** - Jesus as propitiation for our sins

### Authority
- **Romans 13** - Submission to governing authorities
- **1 Peter** - Submission in all relationships
- **Daniel** - Faithfulness under pagan rulers
- **Esther** - Working within systems of power
- **Ephesians** - Order in church, home, and society
