# New Testament Quick Reference

A one-page overview of all 27 New Testament books.

## The Gospels

| Book | Summary | Key Theme | Key Verse | Top Application |
|------|---------|-----------|-----------|-----------------|
| [Matthew](../new-testament/01-matthew.md) | Jesus is the Messiah-King who fulfills Old Testament prophecy and establishes God's kingdom. | Messiah-King | Matt 28:18-20 | Make disciples and live out kingdom ethics |
| [Mark](../new-testament/02-mark.md) | Fast-paced account of Jesus as the powerful Son of God who came as suffering Servant. | Suffering Servant | Mark 10:45 | Serve others sacrificially like Jesus |
| [Luke](../new-testament/03-luke.md) | Jesus is the compassionate Savior for all people, especially the marginalized and outcast. | Savior for All | Luke 19:10 | Welcome the marginalized and seek the lost |
| [John](../new-testament/04-john.md) | Jesus is the eternal Word made flesh who gives eternal life to all who believe. | Eternal Life | John 3:16 | Abide in Christ—remain connected to Him daily |

## History

| Book | Summary | Key Theme | Key Verse | Top Application |
|------|---------|-----------|-----------|-----------------|
| [Acts](../new-testament/05-acts.md) | The Holy Spirit empowers the church to witness from Jerusalem to Rome despite persecution. | Spirit's Power | Acts 1:8 | Be a witness powered by the Holy Spirit |

## Paul's Letters

| Book | Summary | Key Theme | Key Verse | Top Application |
|------|---------|-----------|-----------|-----------------|
| [Romans](../new-testament/06-romans.md) | Salvation is by grace through faith alone; justification brings transformation. | Justification | Rom 1:16-17 | Rest in grace; don't try to earn God's favor |
| [1 Corinthians](../new-testament/07-1-corinthians.md) | Address church divisions and disorders by pursuing love and spiritual maturity. | Church Unity | 1 Cor 13:13 | Let love be your highest priority |
| [2 Corinthians](../new-testament/08-2-corinthians.md) | Paul defends his ministry, showing strength in weakness and God's comfort in suffering. | Comfort | 2 Cor 1:3-4 | Comfort others with the comfort you've received |
| [Galatians](../new-testament/09-galatians.md) | Justification comes through faith in Christ alone, not works; believers are free from legalism. | Freedom | Gal 5:1 | Stand firm in freedom—reject legalism |
| [Ephesians](../new-testament/10-ephesians.md) | God's eternal plan unites believers in Christ with every spiritual blessing. | Unity in Christ | Eph 2:8-10 | Live out your identity in Christ |
| [Philippians](../new-testament/11-philippians.md) | Joy and contentment in Christ despite circumstances through humility and partnership. | Joy | Phil 4:4 | Rejoice always—choose joy regardless of circumstances |
| [Colossians](../new-testament/12-colossians.md) | Christ is supreme over all creation; believers are complete in Him. | Christ's Supremacy | Col 1:15-18 | Set your mind on things above, not earthly things |
| [1 Thessalonians](../new-testament/13-1-thessalonians.md) | Living faithfully while awaiting Christ's return with hope and holiness. | Second Coming | 1 Thess 4:16-17 | Live for eternity—Christ is coming back |
| [2 Thessalonians](../new-testament/14-2-thessalonians.md) | Stand firm in truth, work faithfully, and await Christ's return with perseverance. | Perseverance | 2 Thess 3:3 | Keep working faithfully while waiting for Christ |
| [1 Timothy](../new-testament/15-1-timothy.md) | Instructions for church leadership, sound doctrine, and godly conduct. | Church Order | 1 Tim 3:15 | Pursue godliness and guard sound doctrine |
| [2 Timothy](../new-testament/16-2-timothy.md) | Paul's final charge to Timothy: guard the gospel and endure hardship faithfully. | Endurance | 2 Tim 3:16-17 | Fan into flame your gift—don't be ashamed |
| [Titus](../new-testament/17-titus.md) | Grace teaches believers to live godly lives and be devoted to good works. | Good Works | Titus 2:11-12 | Let grace teach you to live rightly |
| [Philemon](../new-testament/18-philemon.md) | Paul appeals for forgiveness and reconciliation between master and former slave as brothers. | Reconciliation | Philem 1:16 | Forgive radically and see people as family in Christ |

## General Letters

| Book | Summary | Key Theme | Key Verse | Top Application |
|------|---------|-----------|-----------|-----------------|

| [1 John](../new-testament/23-1-john.md) | Assurance of salvation through walking in light, loving others, and believing in Jesus. | Assurance | 1 John 4:19 | Love others because God first loved you |
| [2 John](../new-testament/24-2-john.md) | Walk in truth and love while guarding against false teaching. | Truth & Love | 2 John 1:6 | Balance truth and love—don't compromise either |
| [3 John](../new-testament/25-3-john.md) | Commendation for hospitality and faithfulness; warning against controlling leaders. | Hospitality | 3 John 1:8 | Practice hospitality and support gospel workers |
| [Jude](../new-testament/26-jude.md) | Contend for the faith against false teachers who distort grace. | Contend | Jude 1:3 | Defend the faith while showing mercy wisely |
