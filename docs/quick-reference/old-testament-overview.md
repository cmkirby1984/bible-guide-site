# Old Testament Quick Reference

A one-page overview of all 39 Old Testament books.

## The Law (Pentateuch)

| Book | Summary | Key Theme | Key Verse | Top Application |
|------|---------|-----------|-----------|-----------------|
| [Genesis](../old-testament/01-genesis.md) | God creates the world and humanity, then calls Abraham to establish a covenant people through whom all nations will be blessed. | Beginnings | Gen 1:1 | Trust God's sovereignty even in difficult circumstances |
| [Exodus](../old-testament/02-exodus.md) | God delivers Israel from Egyptian slavery, establishes His covenant at Sinai, and dwells among them in the tabernacle. | Redemption | Ex 20:2 | Remember your redemption from spiritual bondage |
| [Leviticus](../old-testament/03-leviticus.md) | God instructs His redeemed people how to worship Him in holiness through sacrifices, priests, and holy living. | Holiness | Lev 19:2 | Pursue holiness in every area of life |
| [Numbers](../old-testament/04-numbers.md) | Israel's unbelief leads to 40 years of wilderness wandering, but God remains faithful to prepare a new generation. | Faithfulness | Num 23:19 | Don't let unbelief keep you from God's promises |
| [Deuteronomy](../old-testament/05-deuteronomy.md) | Moses calls Israel to love God wholeheartedly, remember His faithfulness, and choose obedience as they enter Canaan. | Covenant Love | Deut 6:4-5 | Love God with all your heart, soul, and strength |

## Historical Books

| Book | Summary | Key Theme | Key Verse | Top Application |
|------|---------|-----------|-----------|-----------------|
| [Joshua](../old-testament/06-joshua.md) | Israel conquers and settles Canaan under Joshua's leadership as God proves faithful to His promises. | Victory | Josh 1:9 | Be strong and courageous—God is with you |
| [Judges](../old-testament/07-judges.md) | Israel cycles through sin, oppression, and deliverance, showing the need for godly leadership and covenant faithfulness. | Cycles of Sin | Judg 21:25 | Don't do what's right in your own eyes—follow God |
| [Ruth](../old-testament/08-ruth.md) | A Moabite woman's loyal devotion leads to blessing and inclusion in the line of David and Jesus. | Redemption | Ruth 1:16 | Practice covenant loyalty even when it's costly |
| [1 Samuel](../old-testament/09-1-samuel.md) | God establishes leaders with hearts devoted to Him, contrasting Saul's disobedience with David's faithfulness. | Leadership | 1 Sam 16:7 | God values your heart over outward appearance |
| [2 Samuel](../old-testament/10-2-samuel.md) | David reigns as king but falls into sin, yet God's grace extends forgiveness while allowing consequences. | Grace & Consequences | 2 Sam 7:12 | Guard against temptation; confess sin quickly |
| [1 Kings](../old-testament/11-1-kings.md) | Solomon's wisdom and temple-building give way to idolatry, causing the kingdom to divide. | Division | 1 Kgs 18:21 | Don't compromise your faith for relationships or success |
| [2 Kings](../old-testament/12-2-kings.md) | Both kingdoms fall into exile due to persistent idolatry despite prophetic warnings. | Judgment | 2 Kgs 17:13 | Heed God's warnings before it's too late |
| [1 Chronicles](../old-testament/13-1-chronicles.md) | Post-exilic retelling emphasizes David's reign and temple preparations to encourage returning exiles. | Worship | 1 Chr 16:11 | Prioritize worship and make it central to your life |
| [2 Chronicles](../old-testament/14-2-chronicles.md) | Kings who seek God prosper while those who abandon Him face disaster, ending with hope of restoration. | Seeking God | 2 Chr 7:14 | Humble yourself, pray, and seek God's face daily |
| [Ezra](../old-testament/15-ezra.md) | God brings exiles back to rebuild the temple despite opposition; Ezra leads spiritual renewal through Scripture. | Restoration | Ezra 7:10 | Devote yourself to studying, obeying, and teaching God's Word |
| [Nehemiah](../old-testament/16-nehemiah.md) | Nehemiah rebuilds Jerusalem's walls through prayer and action, then leads covenant renewal. | Prayer & Action | Neh 4:14 | Combine prayer with diligent work |
| [Esther](../old-testament/17-esther.md) | Jewish queen courageously saves her people from genocide, revealing God's hidden providence. | Providence | Est 4:14 | You are where you are "for such a time as this" |

## Wisdom Literature

| Book | Summary | Key Theme | Key Verse | Top Application |
|------|---------|-----------|-----------|-----------------|
| [Job](../old-testament/18-job.md) | A righteous man endures inexplicable suffering, learning that God's wisdom transcends human understanding. | Suffering | Job 13:15 | Trust God even without understanding why you suffer |
| [Psalms](../old-testament/19-psalms.md) | Israel's inspired hymnal expressing the full range of human emotions before a faithful God. | Worship | Ps 23:1 | Pray the Psalms—use them to express your heart to God |
| [Proverbs](../old-testament/20-proverbs.md) | Practical wisdom for godly living begins with fearing the LORD and applies to every area of life. | Wisdom | Prov 1:7 | Control your tongue and choose friends wisely |
| [Ecclesiastes](../old-testament/21-ecclesiastes.md) | Life without God is meaningless; purpose is found in fearing God and keeping His commandments. | Meaning | Eccl 12:13 | Don't seek ultimate satisfaction in earthly pursuits |
| [Song of Solomon](../old-testament/22-song-of-solomon.md) | Celebration of romantic and sexual love within marriage as God's beautiful gift. | Marital Love | Song 8:6-7 | Honor marital intimacy and cultivate romance |

## Major Prophets

| Book | Summary | Key Theme | Key Verse | Top Application |
|------|---------|-----------|-----------|-----------------|
| [Isaiah](../old-testament/23-isaiah.md) | God's holy judgment on sin and glorious promise of salvation through the suffering Messiah. | Messiah | Isa 53:5 | Find comfort in God's promises and sovereignty |
| [Jeremiah](../old-testament/24-jeremiah.md) | The weeping prophet warns Judah of coming judgment while promising a New Covenant. | New Covenant | Jer 29:11 | Seek heart transformation, not just external religion |
| [Lamentations](../old-testament/25-lamentations.md) | Poetic mourning over Jerusalem's destruction, yet finding hope in God's unfailing mercies. | Grief & Hope | Lam 3:22-23 | God's mercies are new every morning |
| [Ezekiel](../old-testament/26-ezekiel.md) | God's glory departs from the temple due to sin but promises to give His people new hearts. | God's Glory | Ezek 36:26 | Pursue God's transforming presence in your life |
| [Daniel](../old-testament/27-daniel.md) | Faithful living in exile demonstrates God's sovereignty over all kingdoms and coming kingdom. | Sovereignty | Dan 2:21 | Maintain integrity even in hostile environments |

## Minor Prophets

| Book | Summary | Key Theme | Key Verse | Top Application |
|------|---------|-----------|-----------|-----------------|
| [Hosea](../old-testament/28-hosea.md) | God's unfailing love for unfaithful Israel pictured through Hosea's marriage to an adulteress. | God's Love | Hos 6:6 | Return to God—His love never fails |
| [Joel](../old-testament/29-joel.md) | Locust plague pictures coming judgment, but God promises restoration and His Spirit. | The Spirit | Joel 2:28 | Respond to crises by turning to God in repentance |
| [Amos](../old-testament/30-amos.md) | God demands justice and righteousness, rejecting worship divorced from ethics. | Justice | Amos 5:24 | Let justice roll like a river—care for the poor |
| [Obadiah](../old-testament/31-obadiah.md) | Edom judged for pride and violence against Judah; God will judge all nations. | Pride | Obad 1:3 | Guard against pride—it leads to destruction |
| [Jonah](../old-testament/32-jonah.md) | Reluctant prophet learns God's compassion extends to all nations, even enemies. | God's Mercy | Jonah 4:2 | Extend grace to those you find difficult to love |
| [Micah](../old-testament/33-micah.md) | God requires justice, mercy, and humble walking with Him, promising a Messiah from Bethlehem. | True Religion | Mic 6:8 | Act justly, love mercy, walk humbly with God |
| [Nahum](../old-testament/34-nahum.md) | God's judgment on Nineveh shows He avenges evil and cares for those who trust Him. | God's Justice | Nah 1:7 | God is your refuge—trust His justice |
| [Habakkuk](../old-testament/35-habakkuk.md) | Prophet questions God's justice but learns to live by faith and rejoice in God alone. | Faith | Hab 2:4 | Live by faith even when circumstances are difficult |
| [Zephaniah](../old-testament/36-zephaniah.md) | The Day of the Lord brings judgment on complacency but salvation for the humble remnant. | Day of the Lord | Zeph 3:17 | Don't be spiritually complacent—seek the Lord now |
| [Haggai](../old-testament/37-haggai.md) | Prioritize God's house over personal comfort; consider your ways and rebuild. | Priorities | Hag 1:7 | Put God's kingdom first in your priorities |
| [Zechariah](../old-testament/38-zechariah.md) | Visions and prophecies encourage temple rebuilding and point to the coming Messiah-King. | Messianic Hope | Zech 9:9 | Depend on God's Spirit, not human strength |
| [Malachi](../old-testament/39-malachi.md) | God confronts His people's spiritual apathy and calls them to faithful worship and giving. | Honor God | Mal 3:10 | Give God your best, not your leftovers |
