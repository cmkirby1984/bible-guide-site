## üìö What's Included for Each Book

Every book overview contains:

1. **Book Overview** - Author, date, historical context, original audience, purpose, and genre
2. **One-Sentence Summary** - Concise, memorable overview
3. **Book Structure** - Outline showing major divisions and flow
4. **Key Verses** - 2-3 most important verses with full text and significance
5. **Key Themes & Messages** - 3-5 major theological themes and the central message
6. **Practical Application** - Concrete applications for daily living and spiritual growth
7. **Recommended Commentaries** - Curated resources from beginner to academic level

## üöÄ How to Use This Guide

Once you've added your content:

- **Sequential Study**: Read through the books in order to understand the biblical narrative
- **Topical Study**: Search themes across Scripture using your indexes
- **Book Study**: Deep dive into individual books with overviews and themes as your foundation
- **Devotional Reading**: Use the central messages for daily reflection and application
